Sanskriti The Gurukul


Sanskriti The Gurukul is a day boarding school located in the outskirts of Guwahati, India.


The school follows the ICSE and ISC curriculum and was inaugurated by S.K. Sinha as North-East India's first day boarding school. It was established based on the ideas of Asutosh Aggarwal.
